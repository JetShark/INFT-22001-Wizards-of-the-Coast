Magic: The Gathering Rules & Formats

Basic Rules

Magic: The Gathering is a collectible card game played with a deck of cards. Players take turns casting spells, summoning creatures, and using various cards to defeat their opponents.

Formats

There are various formats in which Magic: The Gathering can be played. Some popular formats include:

  • Standard
  • Modern
  • Commander
